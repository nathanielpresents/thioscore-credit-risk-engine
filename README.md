# 🏦 EthioScore Engine: Alternative Credit Risk Infrastructure

An automated, data-driven underwriting prototype designed to mitigate the "thin-file" credit gap in emerging financial ecosystems. This system utilizes mobile money transactional markers, decentralized cooperative histories, and localized asset proxies to evaluate risk profiles while programmatically enforcing compliance mapping.

---

## 📌 Project Overview & Thesis
Traditional underwriting frameworks remain structurally tethered to physical real estate collateral and formal payroll tracking. This platform shifts the paradigm toward transactional velocity, enabling financial institutions to safely tap into unbanked demographics while maintaining robust capital protections.

## 🛠️ Core Functional Modules

### 1. Interactive Credit Score Simulator (`Live Scorer`)
* Simulates real-time underwriting scenarios on a transparent **300 to 850 scoring range**[cite: 1].
* Calculates dynamic risk actions (e.g., routing borderline profiles to **Manual Review** or modifying maximum exposure caps)[cite: 1].
* Displays a granular feature weight attribution matrix for complete model explainability[cite: 1].

### 2. Institutional Portfolio Analytics (`Applicant Pipeline`)
* Evaluates aggregated historical data pipelines over a mock cohort of **300 concurrent applicants**[cite: 1].
* Computes live macro metrics: total book valuation, approval yield ratios, and mean cohort scoring metrics[cite: 1].
* Generates geographic credit concentration charts to protect bank balances against localized economic shocks[cite: 1].

### 3. Automated Regulatory Engine (`NBE Compliance`)
* Hardcodes baseline credit limits, risk classifications, and expected loss provisioning weights directly into the data layer in strict alignment with **National Bank of Ethiopia (NBE) Directive SBB/80/2022 (Articles 5, 6, and 9)**[cite: 1].

## 📊 Algorithmic Fairness & Parity Audit
The platform incorporates an active demographic audit layer tracking performance variations across Urban vs. Rural divisions and diverse Age groups, prompting prescriptive data-science solutions when structural approval variances are spotted across thin-file segments[cite: 1].
